# AFFAIR REVEALED

## Core Concept
Discovery of infidelity that shatters protagonist's reality and trust. May be the central mystery or catalyst for darker revelations. Affair itself may be cover for worse crimes, or lead protagonist to discover other secrets while investigating betrayal.

## Key Characteristics
- Protagonist discovers or suspects partner's infidelity
- Evidence gathering (texts, credit card statements, suspicious behavior)
- Confrontation and denial or confession
- Identity of affair partner may be shocking (friend, family member, colleague)
- May reveal deeper secrets or crimes beyond infidelity
- Protagonist must decide whether to stay or leave
- Affects children, finances, social circle

## Narrative Use
**Setup:** Happy marriage with small cracks
**Escalation:** Suspicion → investigation → discovery → confrontation
**Payoff:** Truth revealed (simple affair or gateway to darker secrets)

## Compatible Elements
✅ Gaslighting, financial control, secrets, murder-for-insurance, love triangle gone wrong
⚠️ Making affair sympathetic vs. condemning, gender dynamics
❌ Trivializing betrayal, easy forgiveness without work

## Genre Fit
- Psychological thriller: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- Domestic thriller: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

## Example Application
Protagonist suspects husband of affair, investigates. Discovers he's having affair with her best friend. But deeper investigation reveals they're embezzling from her business together, or planning to kill her for insurance. Affair is part of larger conspiracy against her.
