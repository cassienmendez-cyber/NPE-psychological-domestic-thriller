# STOLEN IDENTITY

## Core Concept
Someone is actively taking over protagonist's identity—financial, social, digital, or literal replacement. Protagonist's life is being systematically stolen while they're still alive. Creates horror of being erased and replaced.

## Key Characteristics
- Identity thief copies appearance, mannerisms, life details
- Opens accounts, makes purchases, commits crimes in protagonist's name
- May physically replace protagonist in their relationships/job
- Digital identity theft (social media, accounts, data)
- Protagonist's attempts to prove identity fail
- Others believe the impostor over real person

## Narrative Use
**Setup:** Small identity compromises escalate
**Escalation:** Systematic takeover, protagonist loses credibility, impostor gains ground
**Payoff:** Protagonist reclaims identity, exposes impostor

## Compatible Elements
✅ Parasitic friend, doppelganger, financial crime, single-white-female dynamics
⚠️ Legal system portrayal, making takeover believable
❌ Easy police solution, unrealistic identity verification

## Genre Fit
- Psychological thriller: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- Domestic thriller: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

## Example Application
Protagonist discovers someone has stolen her identity online, then realizes the person is copying her in real life too—got same haircut, befriended her friends, applied to her job. The impostor is systematically replacing her in her own life, and when she tries to prove who she is, the impostor has seeded doubt about her stability. Must expose impostor before complete replacement.
