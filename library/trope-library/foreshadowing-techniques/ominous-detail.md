# OMINOUS DETAIL

## Core Concept
Small, seemingly insignificant detail mentioned early that takes on sinister meaning later. Subtle foreshadowing that rewards attentive readers on re-read. Creates sense that danger was always present, protagonist just didn't see it.

## Key Characteristics
- Detail seems minor or atmospheric initially
- Protagonist notices but dismisses it
- Later events reveal its significance
- Examples: broken lock, missing photo, overheard conversation, repeated phrase, specific object
- Creates "I should have known" feeling for protagonist
- Reader may catch it first, creating dramatic irony

## Narrative Use
**Setup:** Plant detail naturally in early chapters
**Escalation:** May repeat or reference detail subtly
**Payoff:** Detail's significance revealed, recontextualizes earlier scenes

## Compatible Elements
✅ Mystery plots, careful plotting, clue planting, re-readable stories
⚠️ Not making it too obvious or too obscure
❌ Planting red herring that never pays off

## Genre Fit
- Psychological thriller: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- Domestic thriller: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

## Example Application
Chapter 2: Protagonist notices new neighbor's basement window is covered with blackout curtains, thinks it's odd but dismisses it. Chapter 28: Discovers neighbor has been holding someone captive in basement. Detail was clue to hidden horror protagonist walked past daily.
